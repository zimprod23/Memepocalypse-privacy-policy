Introduction

Welcome to Memepocalypse! We value your privacy and are dedicated to providing a transparent experience. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when using our app. By using Memepocalypse, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Memepocalypse does not collect or store any personal data. All memes, and app settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to external servers. We respect your privacy and ensure that no data leaves your device.

Login and Authentication

Memepocalypse offers users the option to log in or sign up using their email address or through anonymous login. When users choose to log in with an email, we store their email address to provide login credentials and facilitate access to their account. If users opt for anonymous login, we do not collect or store any personal information. In both cases, no data is shared with external servers or third parties.

Our app includes banner ads and rewarded ads. These ads are served by third-party providers, which may collect non-personally identifiable information for ad targeting. Memepocalypse does not share any user data with ad providers.

Language Support

Memepocalypse is available in three languages: French, Arabic, and English. You can easily switch between languages within the app.
